# beaker browser

## `install beaker`, *~15 mins*

- grab the installer from the usb stick or
- download beaker [from here](https://beakerbrowser.com/install)

## `explore beaker`, *~45 mins*

- make sites
- visite sites
- seed sites
- make an editable copy of sites (forking)
- use beaker internal editor
- browse a dat site history (versioning)
- host a dat
  - hashbase
  - homebase
  - your own laptop forever on
  - [soon](https://github.com/datrs/dat) from your tiny computer (aka
    phones)
